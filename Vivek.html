Best Time to Visit Hubli

The winter season from October to February is the best time to explore Hubli. The weather is pleasant, and you can comfortably visit various tourist sites without any hassle.

Restaurants in Hubli

The Sigdi Veg BBQ

From main course items such as spicy paneer masala to mint wala dal, this incredible restaurant serves an assortment of mouth-watering Indian dishes with a twist.

Barbeque Nation - Hubballi

Indulge in some of the most exquisite vegetarian and non-vegetarian fare. From grilled mushrooms to tandoori chicken, there is no dearth of options here.

Oaks 2.0

Tempt your taste buds with a variety of cuisines served including South Indian, continental, Mexican, and Italian fare. You can also savour a variety of pastries here.

Things to Buy in Hubli

Hubli is a handloom textile hub, offering a wide range of traditional Kannad sarees and lungis. Additionally, you can find steel and iron accessories, and explore shopping malls like Galaxy Mall and Urban Oasis Mall.

Urban Oasis Mall

If you want a luxurious shopping experience or to try a large selection of foreign items, the Urban Oasis Mall on Gokul Road is your best bet. It also features a multiplex cinema and diverse international restaurants.

Conclusion

Hubli, with its blend of culture, scenic views, and modern amenities, offers a memorable experience for all visitors. With well-developed infrastructure and good transportation, exploring the city is both convenient and enjoyable. Plan your visit and enjoy the beautiful landscapes and vibrant culture of Hubli!
